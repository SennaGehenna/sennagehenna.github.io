Creating or joining games

It's up to the players to organize when to play. Ask people if they're interested in joining you on your expedition and see if there's a GM available.

After Action Reports

After a session it is up to the players to write After Action Reports (commonly referred to as AARs). Writing AARs is a very important part of sharing information with the other players in the land of the West Marches. AARs usually contain information on what happened on the mission and often includes the character's outlook on the mission.
Feel free to make this an "In Character" report or an "Out of Character" report.

Common things to include are: locations visited, NPCs encountered, monsters encountered, points of interest, events of interest.

Every Player who writes at least one AAR after a session will be rewarded with 25 gold.

Adventures

Adventures will not take place in town. Explore the wilds and find your story.
The wilds generally, although not always, grow more dangerous the further you travel, so remember:

Running is always an option.

The players have persistent effects on the world and it will dynamically change around them.

Character Creation

You start at Level 1.

Allowed Sources:

  • Player's Handbook
  • Xanathar's Guide to Everything
  • Volo's Guide to Monsters (except the monstrous races)
  • Mordekainen's Tome of Foes
  • Unearthed Arcana
  • Tomb of Annihilation
  • Loxodons

Starting Gold/Equipment:

You can either take starting class+background equipment OR roll for gold. If you roll, tell one of the GMs first.

Exceptions:

The following exceptions exist:
  • The following backgrounds can not be chosen: Hermit, Inheritor, Faction Agent
    • additionally, the outlander background loses a part of its feature that says

      You can find food and fresh water for yourself and up to five other people each day, provided that the land offers berries, small game, water, and so forth.

  • The following subclasses are restricted:
    • Druid - Circle of Dreams may not be used
    • Warlock - Undead Patreon may not be used
    • Wizard - School of Necromancy may not be used
  • The following spells are restricted:
    • Goodberry consumes its material component
    • Leomund's Tiny Hut consumes its material component
    • Simulacrum may not be used
    • Wish may not be used
    • All spells that raise undead have no functionality, there is no undeath in this campaign
  • The following feats are restricted:
    • Lucky may not be used
    • Feats from UA may not be used
    • The following feats may not be selected through the variant humans ability Feat.
      However, they may be selected when obtaining feats normally:
      • Alert
      • Crossbow Expert
      • Great Weapon Master
      • Heavy Armor Master
      • Polearm Master
      • Sentinel
      • Shield Master
      • Sharpshooter
